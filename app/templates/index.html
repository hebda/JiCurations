Clothing and Living


Ji is a reflection of our love for handcrafted textiles. We are a woman-owned textile studio based out of California, with focus on resist-dyeing and hand block printing. Travels across India, and training with several artisans taught us a thing or two about textile dyeing and printing, and inspired the launch of our home-based studio, where our creations come alive. From designing and sourcing materials, to dyeing and sewing products to a finish, we do it all right here.

We embrace the slow nature of handcrafting culture, and craft our clothing and home products with great attention to detail, design and quality.

We look forward to sharing this space and our journey with you soon. In the meantime, follow our stories on our social media (scroll to the very end!).
