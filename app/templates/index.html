About Ji

Specializing in handmade, sustainable, and regional products


Ji is a reflection of our love for all things handcrafted, and a passion to play a part in providing livelihood to low-income and marginalized artisan groups of India.

Our clothing, accessories and home decor are crafted with great attention to detail, quality and design, using handloom fabrics from weaver communities across the country.
