About Ji

Specializing in handmade, sustainable, and regional products


At Ji, we meticulously handcraft and handpick an eclectic mix of products including home decor, clothing and other utilitarian creations, in collaboration with grassroots organizations and artisans.

In addition to making a lot of products ourselves, we strive to support other small artisan groups that keep traditional Indian art and craft alive, by handpicking materials and products from them.

Each listing has under its description, the materials used and recommended care. If you have further questions, please send us a message and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Most of our products are unique pieces, which means they are not made to order. However, please do let us know if there is a product that you would like to be re-made just for you. We will try our best to fit your request!
